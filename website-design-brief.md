Impact highlights:
●+12–25% retention

●+20–25% first-contact resolution

●–15–20% complaints

●+15–20 pp uplift in NPS/CSAT

●20–30% increase in CX/CS operational efficiency

●Increase in NRR / CLV


5. Expectation Management Framework (EMM)

 Maps customer expectations across journeys/touchpoints to organizational delivery competencies, surfacing specific gaps for prioritized interventions.
Impact highlights:
